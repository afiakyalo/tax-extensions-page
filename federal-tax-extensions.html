Key Reminders

Extension forms must be filed by the original due date

Pay estimated tax by original due date to avoid interest charges

Penalties may be waived for reasonable cause

Interest accrues on unpaid taxes from original due date

This matrix covers federal tax extensions. State filing requirements may vary. Consult a tax professional for specific situations.
